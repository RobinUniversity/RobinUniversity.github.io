WELCOME TO CYCLE BELFAST NI

The best site for all cyclists keen to explore Belfast
Where that be for commuting to work or exploring national landmarks.



When working to develop this webpage, we knew that Belfast has a highly dense population according to the census during the 2010's.They also found that less than 1% of the population cycle to work daily. Despite the vast opportunity there is to do so.

The aim of CycleBelfastNI is to increase sustainable travel and reducing GHG emmissions is a goal across most cities. We aim to become a useful tool for locals and tourists visiting the city to find safe cycle routes whether it be for commuting to work or leisure purposes.

About Us

At CycleBelfastNI, we are the leading source of route informations across Northern Ireland with 50,000 live users monthly. We aim to improve the leisure and tourism markets within Belfast by utilising the city's landmarks and providing the user with smooth route direction to optimise cycling within the city.
